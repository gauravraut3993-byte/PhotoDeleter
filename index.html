Privacy Policy for Photo Deleter and Sorter app

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection: FreePhotoSorter does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal photos or videos to any external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions: The app requires access to your Photo Library solely to provide the sorting and deleting functionality you request.

3. Third Parties: We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to those transactions.
